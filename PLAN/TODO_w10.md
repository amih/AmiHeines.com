## Sub-growth (over-cap, 5h) — user-deliberate per 2026-05-01

### Personal follow-up to challenge attendees who showed cohort interest (5h)
Per Cochrane discipline (`PLAN §3` "no 1:1 sales calls") — these are **text follow-ups**, not phone/Zoom calls.

- [ ] **Day 5 (Fri) end-of-session:** identify the 5–10 attendees who asked engaged technical questions, finished homework, or stayed late. These are the cohort prospects.
- [ ] **Sat morning:** personal text email to each (not a template) — reference something specific they said/built during the week, ask 1 specific question about their goals, mention the cohort offer link in a P.S. only.
- [ ] **Sun:** any positive replies → personal answer addressing their specific situation. NO phone call offer.
- [ ] **Mon (W11) early:** any silent prospects → 1 follow-up email asking "did the cohort feel like the right next step? Either way, I'd love to know what you decided so I can adjust." Honest curiosity, not pressure.
- [ ] Track conversion attribution: which cohort sales came from which personal follow-ups → `PLAN/cohort_sales_attribution.md`. Used for Cohort #2 calibration.
