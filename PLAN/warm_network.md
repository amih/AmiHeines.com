## What counts as "warm"

A name belongs in this file ONLY if at least one is true:
- You have exchanged ≥1 private message (email, DM, voice, in-person) with them in the last 24 months
- They follow you on a platform AND you've had any real-name interaction
- They were a paying client / collaborator / co-attendee / mentor / mentee at any point
- They are referrers — you have a real relationship with them, and they have many connections to Garnon-shaped people (e.g., bootcamp instructors, dev-podcast hosts, Discord-community admins)

A name does **not** belong here if:
- You only know them through their public broadcasts (their YouTube, their Twitter, their HN comments)
- The relationship is one-directional (you read them; they don't know you exist)
- You only "know of" them
- They are warm to a *different* persona than Garnon

## How to use this list (W08–W10)

This file is **not** a cold-outreach list. It's a *who do I personally invite* list.

- **W08:** soft mention in personal DMs to 5–8 names that the challenge is coming.
- **W09:** when the challenge cart opens, send each name a short personal note: *"You crossed my mind for this — no pressure, here's the link."* Not a pitch — an invitation.
- **W10 mid-week:** if attendance is below stretch, follow up with referrers asking for a single share.
- **W10 Day 5 → Day 7:** for cohort sales, the warm-network names that bought a challenge ticket get a 1-line nudge about the cohort. The ones who didn't buy a ticket get nothing — they self-selected out.

**Do not spam.** The whole value of "warm" is that one personal note converts at 10–30%. Spammy mass-CC outreach destroys both the relationships and the conversion rate.
